A Greeting

Oct 9, 2025

To anyone reading this: Welcome!

I’ve been toying with the idea of starting a blog for a while, but I never quite knew where to start-so I kept putting it off.

Well, I’ve started.

I have a simple plan for this: to make it my social media replacement. I really, truly dislike the way modern social media has been going for a while now, but I still enjoy keeping up with people I don’t often get to see in person.

This blog will be a place for TILs (see Josh Branchaud’s TIL repo), micro-blogging, and longer posts. The longer posts will most likely come from lesson plans or other larger notes and projects. I’m planning to take a month-long deep dive into Biblical canonization, so expect to see that sometime next month.

Thank you for indulging me-and I’ll see you in the next one.

God bless,

Matthew
